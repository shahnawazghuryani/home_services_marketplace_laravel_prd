# Urdu Video Storyboards And Captions

## How to use this file
- Har video ko vertical mobile-friendly format mein bana sakte hain: 1080x1920.
- Tone simple, warm, aur trust-building rakhein.
- Har screen ke liye short Urdu captions niche diye gaye hain.
- Agar voiceover use na karna ho to captions ko full-screen text ke taur par bhi use kiya ja sakta hai.

## Caption export format
Use this simple subtitle style:
- One line max 8 to 12 words
- Two lines only when needed
- White text with dark shadow
- Key words highlight with warm yellow
